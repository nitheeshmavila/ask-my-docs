Ask My Docs — Product Roadmap
Vision
A production-grade clinical knowledge assistant that answers questions grounded strictly in large-scale medical literature — PubMed abstracts, CDC/WHO guidelines, FDA drug labels — with every claim traced to a source. Built to demonstrate that RAG is only meaningful at the scale where retrieval is a real problem.

Final Destination: Healthcare Knowledge Assistant
The product
A clinical Q&A system where doctors, researchers, or medical students upload or query against a pre-loaded corpus of authoritative medical literature and get answers that are:

Grounded — every claim traced to a specific source chunk
Cited — exact document, section, and passage referenced
Honest — system says "not found in literature" rather than hallucinating

Example query the final system handles

"What is the recommended first-line treatment for community-acquired pneumonia in adults with penicillin allergy?"
Returns an answer citing the exact CDC/IDSA guideline passage — not general LLM knowledge.
